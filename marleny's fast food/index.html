Marleny's fast food, local and fresh traditional foods!

Founded in the early 2000s by Marleny, many from across Belize come and enjoy cultural foods of Belize using only the freshest ingredients, made as one orders it! With hours of careful preparation, Marleny's fast food ensures your meal is perfectly crafted with maximum delicacy!

Opening Hours

Our humble fast food stall opens from Thursday to Monday at 5PM to 12AM, making it a perfect place for dinner or a late-night snack! We take orders via call-ins too, allowing you to order straight from home!



More about us

Beginning as a small area in the San Ignacio Marketplace, Ms. Marleny Espinoza soon expanded her business venture into something much bigger. Though she chose to remain as a small foodstall in downtown San Ignacio, the influence of her amazing cooking soon spread like wildfire in 2013; giving her the fame she deserves today!
